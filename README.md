📊 Business Insights 360 – Power BI Dashboard

A cross-functional Business Intelligence solution built using Power BI to deliver actionable insights across Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, and Executive operations.

🧩 Business Challenge

Atliq Hardware experienced rapid growth across global markets, which introduced several analytical bottlenecks:

Fragmented data across multiple systems

Lack of a unified analytics platform for leadership

Difficulty in identifying high-value customers and products

Limited visibility into forecast accuracy and operational efficiency

Dependence on static, non-interactive reporting

These issues made it challenging to answer critical business questions such as:

Which markets drive the highest revenue?

What products contribute most to profitability?

How reliable are sales forecasts?

Which cost components impact margins?

🎯 Objective

Design and develop an interactive Power BI dashboard that centralizes business data and enables stakeholders to:

Track financial performance

Analyze customer and sales behavior

Evaluate marketing effectiveness

Monitor supply chain forecasting

Access executive-level KPIs

📦 Dataset Description

The dataset simulates real-world operations of Atliq Hardware and includes:

Sales transactions

Customer and product data

Market segmentation

Forecast and actual performance

Cost and financial metrics

⚠️ Dataset sourced from Codebasics BI course (not publicly shareable)

🧹 Data Preparation

Data cleaning and transformation were performed using Power Query, including:

Removing duplicates and null values

Standardizing naming conventions

Fixing inconsistent date formats

Creating hierarchies (market/product)

Correcting data types

Building calculated columns

🧠 Data Modeling Approach

A Multi-Fact Star Schema was implemented for scalability and performance.

📊 Dashboard Coverage

The solution includes multiple analytical views:

Home Dashboard – Overview of KPIs

Finance View – Profit & Loss, cost breakdown

Sales View – Revenue trends, customer insights

Marketing View – Campaign and segment analysis

Supply Chain View – Forecast vs actual performance

Executive View – High-level strategic metrics

🔍 Key Insights Generated

A small group of customers contributes a major share of revenue

Certain product categories yield significantly higher margins

Forecast accuracy varies notably across regions

Operational expenses have a direct impact on profitability

✅ Business Impact

The dashboard enables:

Real-time performance monitoring

Identification of high-profit segments

Improved forecasting accuracy

Faster, data-driven decision-making
